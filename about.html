The History of the Aesthetic and Our Brand

About alterNet

alterNet is a revival brand inspired by the bold optimism of Y2K Futurism and the glossy, vibrant world of Frutiger Aero.

Emerging at the turn of the millennium, Y2K Futurism was a vision of the future that embraced sleek tech, silver fashions, translucent interfaces, and cyber-optimism. With its spiky updos, Oakleys, and digital dreams, it captured a world excited—if a little anxious—about the 21st century. But as time passed, this aesthetic gave way to something smoother and more organic: Frutiger Aero.

Named after the Frutiger font and Windows Aero interface, Frutiger Aero dominated the mid-2000s with glassy textures, floating bubbles, fish, gradients, and a blend of nature with digital innovation. It brought humanity and harmony into the sleek designs of the era, crafting a futuristic vibe that felt both high-tech and grounded.

alterNet blends the edginess of Y2K with the airy beauty of Frutiger Aero—reclaiming the forgotten future we were promised. Through refurbished tech, accessories, and fashion, we’re bringing back the future, one glossy button at a time.
